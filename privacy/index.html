Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Bright Bail Bonds ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you provide when contacting us for bail bond services, including names, contact information, and case details necessary to process bail bonds. We collect this information solely to provide our bail bond services and communicate with you about your case.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used to process bail bonds, communicate about your case, comply with legal requirements, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Confidentiality

We understand the sensitive nature of bail bond services. All client information is treated with strict confidentiality and is only shared as required by law or as necessary to process your bail bond.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at info@brightbailbonds.com or visit our privacy policy.
